how to play

use the paddle to prevent the ball from hitting the ground. the paddle can be controlled using the left and right arrow keys.

points accumulate the longer you play; every 10 seconds, you are awarded with 10 points.

each game starts with 5 lives, indicated by the hearts in the top right-hand corner. a life is lost each time the ball hits the ground.

the object of the game is to accumulate as many points as possible before your lives are depleted.
